Mahalunge:A picturesque suburb located on the outskirts of Pune, Mahalunge has emerged as a popular real estate destination in recent years. Pimpri:A historic industrial city located on the outskirts of Pune, Pimpri is rapidly transforming into a modern, well-planned urban center. The area offers a range of affordable housing options, making it an attractive location for first-time homebuyers and those seeking affordable housing.

Manjari:A fast-growing suburb located on the eastern side of Pune, Manjari has emerged as a popular location for real estate development in recent years. The area offers a mix of affordable and mid-range properties, catering to a diverse range of budgets and preferences.

Mamurdi:Located in the western part of Pune, Mamurdi is a quiet, residential suburb that has witnessed steady real estate growth in recent years. The area offers a mix of affordable and premium properties, catering to a wide range of budgets and preferences. With its well-planned infrastructure and serene environment, Mamurdi is a popular choice for those seeking a peaceful, yet well-connected, lifestyle.
