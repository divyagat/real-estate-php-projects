Magarpatta City Group, a self-sustained, modern, eco-sensitive perspective to urban living, is a vibrant community of happy families, living peacefully in a natural ambience. The brand essence of the group is based on three main pillars - People, Purpose and Prosperity.

The journey began with the creation of the 430-acre Magarpatta City, an inclusive growth model by partnering with 235 land-owning farmers. Post that, this journey has had landmark projects under its belt ranging from the beautiful Nanded City, Pune known for being the best township projects in Pune to Seasons Mall and now launching the new residential project in Loni Kalbhor, Pune in form of the spectacular Riverview City. Today this legacy is of 22 glorious years that focused upon sustainable and inclusive development has enhanced the lifestyles of 20,000+ families.
