WELCOME TO NeoLiv Khopoli Plots

NeoLiv Khopoli Plots is a luxury residential plotted development nestled in the scenic landscape of Khopoli. The plots are spacious and come with ample modern amenities in the evolving locale just hours away from Mumbai. The amenities include lifts, parking spaces, a lobby, dedicated security, CCTV, ample open spaces, green gardens, a kids’ play area, and more. Khopoli is a nature-infused locality set to extraordinary growth as the Navi Mumbai International Airport (NMIA) is less than an hour away. The social infrastructure is booming, as it includes Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital, Yak Public School, Shree Raj Educational Centre, Sai Clinic, Jakhotia Nursing Home, Paranjape Surgical and Maternity Hospital, Food Laza Court Mall, Susan Mall, St. Wilfred’s College, etc. The Mumbai Pune Expressway, the JNPT Road, Mumbai–Pune Link Road (MPLR), Panvel Railway Station, Khopoli Railway Station, and the Navi Mumbai International Airport are the major commuting hubs from the property. So, live up to the royalty offered by NeoLiv Khopoli Plots, made to upgrade your lifestyle in Khopoli’s growth corridor.
